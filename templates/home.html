About Jovin

Jovin Capsule is a Capsule manufactured by IND-SWIFT. It is commonly used for the diagnosis or treatment of bladder and bowel control problems, irritable bowel syndrome , cramping pain caused by kidney stones and gallstones. It has some side effects such as Fatigue,Dizziness,Dry mouth,Anxiety. The salts Hypericum are involved in the preparation of Jovin Capsule.
